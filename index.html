THREAT ANALYSIS

KNOW YOUR ENEMY.

The alien horde comes in three flavors — each deadlier than the last. Study them or die.

👾
THE RUNNER
THREAT: MODERATE

Fast, relentless, and they swarm in numbers. A single runner is manageable — twelve of them closing in from every direction is another story. Keep strafing.

🤖
THE BRUTE
THREAT: SEVERE

Slow but devastating. A single hit from a Brute takes a serious chunk of your health. Give them space and let your auto-fire whittle them down from a distance.

💀
THE BOSS
THREAT: CRITICAL

Every 10th wave, something much worse shows up. Massive health pools, unpredictable movement, and the ability to end your run in seconds. This is what you've been preparing for.

FIELD INTELLIGENCE

SURVIVE LONGER.

Dispatches from soldiers who made it past Level 1.

// TIP 01

Never stop moving. A stationary target is a dead target — especially when Runners close the gap in less than a second.

// TIP 02

Use the map edges. Corner your enemies into killzones where your auto-fire can reach them before they reach you.

// TIP 03

Prioritize Brutes in mixed waves. One Brute hit costs more health than surviving five Runners. Take them down first.

// TIP 04

Save your best positioning for Wave 10. Boss fights require room to maneuver — don't let yourself get cornered entering the encounter.

// TIP 05

If you're taking the Sniper loadout, practice your dodge rhythm before the bosses. You can't out-DPS a mistake at close range.

// TIP 06

The Demolisher thrives in Level 3 chaos. If you want a challenge, hold it back until The Last Stand where the enemy density justifies the short range.
